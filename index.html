Mahendra Singh Dhoni (born 7 July 1981) is an Indian professional cricketer who plays as a right handed batter and a wicket-keeper.

Captain Cool: Dhoni is known for his calm leadership and led India to multiple ICC trophies.

Wicketkeeper-Batsman: One of the greatest, combining sharp keeping with powerful batting.
